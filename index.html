Weather Forecast- Application Site

The Weather website, able to tell the current weather conditions and temperature of either your current location or a city of your choosing, is an interactive website showcasing the progression of Selena's coding journey. Also showing real-time data, the website features the weather forecast of the selected city over the next five days. Additional features include humidity and wind-speed data and the times of the sunrise and sunset. All times featured are local times according to the timezone region of the user - not the timezone of the searched city. Still early in Selena's the coding process, the website has yet to be updated to a responsive version. Hopefully in the near future, the website can be accessible over handheld devices.
